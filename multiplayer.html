Multiplayer

Imagine that you find yourself in a game.
You do not know the rules of this game.
You do not know the objective of this game.

You do know that sometimes, things hurt you.
That sometimes, things heal you - make you happier, stronger.

You also know that there are other players in this game.
That they too, can hurt
Or heal.

But nobody knows the objective of the game.
Nobody knows the rules of the game.
Nobody knows the solution to the game.

Play.
