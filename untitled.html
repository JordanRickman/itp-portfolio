Remember
That at one time, words did not exist
And yet this world was here
And we lived
And ate
We hunted, we fought
And laughed and danced
And hated and loved
And communicated.

Should we be so surprised, then
When we cannot speak
When we cannot agree
When we cannot be understood
When words fail?

Should we be so surprised, then
When we cannot answer
Questions we ourselves constructed?
When we peer closely and discover
That we cannot even define our terms?

Should we be so surprised
When words break down?
